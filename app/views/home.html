Our Mission

Natural Gas Partners will generate superior risk-adjusted investment returns while forming and maintaining the highest quality industry relationships and will conduct its business according to the highest standards of honesty, integrity and fairness.

Who We Are

Founded in 1988, Natural Gas Partners manages a family of funds that invests private equity capital in oil and gas production, midstream and oilfield services companies. The NGP platform consists of a series of ten private equity funds with cumulative commitments exceeding $10.5 billion since inception.

Natural Gas Partners is part of the investment platform of NGP Energy Capital Management, a premier investment franchise in the natural resources industry which, together with its affiliates, has managed $13 billion in committed capital since inception.
